Crosschecker



Crosschecker will read through inputted text and compare it against your list of incorrect and correct items in your .csv file. Your file should have two columns with the first row being your chosen heading text.

Spreadsheet layout example:

AB
1IncorrectCorrect
2mangermanager
3johnJohn
4key performance indicatorsKPI
5PRpublic relations
6high riskhigh-risk
7..Delete second full stop.
8 :Delete space before colon.
